West Ham United fans invade the field during their losing game.

Garrick Huynh

(Mark Noble yells at a West Ham fan after subduing them as they invaded the pitch)

As the West Hams began to lose during the London Stadium game, a few supporters had decided to run onto the field to confront or disrupt the Burnleys.


One West Ham fan who was nicknamed "Jesus" invaded the pitch and as he got back to the stands, he was yelled at, tripped, punched, and elbowed while on the steps.

Burnley substitutes had let children sit in their seats to avoid fighting in the stands.


West Ham United plan to issue life bans to those who had invaded the pitch.
