Toronto Maple Leafs win over Dallas Stars, overshadowed by loss of Andersen.

Garrick Huynh

(The two Teams logos.)

The Maple Leafs had pulled an astonishing win over the Stars in a 6-5 shootout.


Although they won the match and made the play-offs, the Maple Leafs couldn't leave the match without concern for their Mvp.

Frederik Andersen, their goalie and MVP, had pulled himself out of the net about 30 minutes after taking Alex Radulov's knee to the head, leaving the rest of the team with concern for him.
